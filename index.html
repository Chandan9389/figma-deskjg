eDemand is an all-in-one Flutter handyman app, CodeIgniter admin panel, and Next.js web platform designed to help entrepreneurs, agencies, and developers launch a scalable multi-vendor on-demand service marketplace with zero setup hassle.

Whether you’re starting a handyman booking app, a home services platform, or a complete service on-demand ecosystem, eDemand has everything you need from customizable templates to real-time bookings and multi-language support.

Built for performance and flexibility, this handyman service application delivers native-like speed, a modern UI, and full white-label customization to suit any niche – making it the ideal service booking script for rapid deployment, monetization, and growth.

What’s Included

eDemand offers a robust technical foundation that best suits high-performance service on-demand applications as well as online handyman marketplace platforms:

  • Flutter Mobile Applications: Separate apps for Customers and Service Providers, delivering personalized user journeys.
  • CodeIgniter Panels: Dedicated panels for Service Providers and a Super Admin to efficiently manage and oversee platform activities.
  • NextJS Website: Individual website for Customers, providing optimized user experiences.
  • Comprehensive Documentation: Step-by-step guides for easy setup and installation.
  • Lifetime Free Updates: Stay current with regular feature upgrades and improvements.
  • 6 Months Technical Support: Access to our expert team for any technical assistance.

12+ Reasons to Choose eDemand for Your Handyman Booking Platform

eDemand is a powerful on-demand service app that connects customers with local professionals for home or in-store services like plumbing, salon, cleaning, and more – an all-in-one platform for admins, vendors, and users.

  • Complete Flutter handyman app and CodeIgniter admin to launch your service booking app quickly.
  • Fully functional online handyman script ideal for home services app and multi-vendor startups.
  • Supports unlimited categories with a number of sub-categories for your handyman listing platform, like electricians, carpenters, and more.
  • Responsive and optimized for mobile as a true Flutter Mobile App.
  • Multiple monetization models – admin commissions, subscriptions, and ads.
  • Smooth performance across Android and iOS with this high-speed Flutter booking app.
  • Robust admin panel to manage services, users, deliverable radius, and provider earnings.
  • Built-in RTL and multi-language support for a global service on demand experience.
  • Modular Flutter + CodeIgniter code, easy to customize and whitelabel.
  • Role-based apps for admin, customer, provider, and a full-service booking marketplace.
  • Powerful Admin dashboard to manage provider data and categories.
  • Quick setup with clean code and step-by-step deployment documentation.

Key Features of eDemand – Flutter Handyman App & Service Booking Platform

eDemand comes with feature-rich and monetizable tools to create the most user-friendly on-demand home services app platform.

  • Multi-Vendor Service Marketplace: Launch your own handyman listing platform where multiple service providers can register, manage services, and accept bookings – all from one powerful service booking marketplace.
  • User-Friendly Service Booking Flow: Customers can browse, schedule, and book services such as plumbing, cleaning, or salon appointments in just a few taps.
  • Advanced Search and Filters: Users can find the right service through keyword, category, and location filters.
  • Geo-Based Service Discovery: Real-time location tracking to suggest nearby providers.
  • Dynamic Service Categories & Subcategories: Fully customizable hierarchy to organize various service types.
  • Built-in Wallet System: Track and manage provider earnings, balances, and payouts seamlessly.
  • Schedule Booking & Real-Time Availability: Supports both instant and scheduled bookings.
  • Push Notifications & Alerts: Real-time booking updates, reminders, offers, and alerts.
  • In-App Chat with Providers: Direct communication between users and providers, with block/unblock option.
  • Multi-Payment Gateway Integration: Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal, Xendit, Paystack, Flutterwave, and COD supported.
  • Real-Time Order Tracking: Users can view live status updates from request to completion.
  • Admin Dashboard & Vendor Panel: Manage vendors, users, services, payments, and more.
  • Commission & Subscription Models: Monetize via commissions or vendor subscriptions.
  • Cross-Platform Flutter App: Works on both Android and iOS with responsive design.
  • White Label Ready: Fully customizable branding for agencies and entrepreneurs.
  • Service Area Management: Define deliverable radius and manage multi-city coverage.
  • Flexible Login Options: Phone OTP, Google, Apple for customers; mobile login for providers.
  • Multi-Service Booking: Customers can book multiple services in a single session.
  • Detailed Service Listings: Includes pricing, duration, photos, provider profiles, reviews, FAQs, and more.
  • Custom Job Requests: Users can request custom services or changes with real-time price updates.
  • Hybrid Fulfillment – Store or Doorstep: Providers can offer in-store or at-home visits.
  • Ad Monetization: Integrated ads for customer and provider apps to maximize revenue.
  • Additional Charges: Providers can add extra charges after booking completion.
  • Promo Codes: Providers can create and manage discount codes.
  • Ratings & Reviews: Customers can leave feedback with text and images after booking completion.
  • Multi-Theme Support: Dark and Light mode available for apps and website.
  • Multi-Address Management: Customers can save and manage multiple addresses for service bookings.
  • Work Proof Upload: Providers can upload before/after photos of completed jobs.
  • OTP Verification: Providers must verify via OTP before marking a booking as complete.
  • Robust SEO Options: Dynamic SEO setup for provider and service details.
  • Multi-Language Data Entry: Admin can add content in multiple languages for better customer experience.
  • Blogs: Admin can post blogs with categories for SEO and engagement.
  • FAQs Section: Built-in FAQ manager for quick customer solutions.
  • App Update Management: Notify users of new app versions and enforce force updates if needed.
  • Maintenance Mode: Enable system maintenance with scheduling options.
  • Featured Home Screen Sections: Create dynamic sections like categories, providers, and banners.
  • PWA Support: Website supports Progressive Web App features.
  • External Storage: Option to use Local server or Amazon S3 for file storage.
  • SMS Gateway Integration: Twilio support for OTPs, order updates, and notifications.
  • Admin Gallery: Centralized gallery for managing uploaded files and images.
  • Reschedule or ReBook: Customers can reschedule or rebook services with ease.
  • Custom Notification Sounds: Different sounds for new orders in provider app.
  • Bulk Upload: Manage providers and services quickly with CSV upload.
  • Tax Management: Supports multiple tax types for easy service setup.
  • Automatic Refunds: Gateway payments auto-refunded to bank accounts on cancellations.
  • System Users: Add multiple admin users with permission controls.
